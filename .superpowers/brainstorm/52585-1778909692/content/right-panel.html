What goes in the right-side panel?

Donezo's right panel has a project list + time tracker. Your app needs something domain-specific. Pick the most useful widget column for your dashboard.

A

Sales Activity + Quick Actions

Top: Recent sales (customer name, amount, status) — last 5 transactions today. Bottom: Quick-action buttons — New Sale, Add Booking, Log Expense.

B

Customer Highlights + Overdue Tasks

Top: Customers with stale orders (haven't ordered in X days — uses your existing staleness composable). Bottom: Overdue maintenance tasks list.

C

Today's Route Summary + Quick Actions

Top: Delivery assignments by rider — who has how many stops today. Bottom: Quick-action buttons for the most common daily operations.
